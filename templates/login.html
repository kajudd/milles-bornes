Mille Bornes

The premise of Mille Bornes is that the players are in a road race.

Each race—or hand—is 1000 miles long. The first player to travel this distance by putting down miles cards wins.

Mille Bornes is played with a special deck of cards. Hover over the card to see what it does. There are hazard, remedy, immunity, and distance cards. Each hazard is corrected by a corresponding remedy, and is actually prevented from happening in the first place by a corresponding immunity. The target distance is reached by playing distance cards. Play hazard cards to stop your opponent until they can put down safety or immunity cards and continue.
